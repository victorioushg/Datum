From the Artist

June 15th, 2004

The Iconfactory 200th Icon Set by The Iconfactory

Way back in the last millineum (April 1996) the Iconfactory was launched at a tiny AOL account called icnfactory. We went online with 6 icon sets. Over eight years later we proudly present our 200th collection! As a tribute to our humble beginnings (back when you only needed to click 1024 pixels to make a Mac OS icon) this collection is in the pre-OS X style. It contains hand crafted 32×32 and 16×16 pixel icons so if you view your desktop or Finder at those sizes you see crisp classic style images. However, it is also optimized for Mac OS X with beautiful and unique 128×128 pixel resources and is a complete CandyBar set to give your Mac a modern makeover with a classic twist.

We hope you enjoy our little taste of nostalgia and we’ll see you in a couple of years for the 300th set!
